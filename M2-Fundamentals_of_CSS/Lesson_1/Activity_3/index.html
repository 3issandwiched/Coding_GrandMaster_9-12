My Favourite Cartoon Character

Doraemon

Doraemon is my favourite cartoon character because he is kind, helpful, and always ready to support his friends.

Character Details

  • Name: Doraemon
  • Best Friend: Nobita
  • Favourite Food: Dorayaki
  • Special Power: Future Gadgets

Fun Fact: Doraemon has a magical pocket that stores many useful gadgets.

Doraemon teaches us that friendship, kindness, and problem-solving are very important.
